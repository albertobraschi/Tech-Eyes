Armasight

The night vision industry has recently seen the introduction of Armasight. This relatively new company has been in business only since the middle of 2012, by Christmas of 2013 Armasight had accomplished impressive sales figures with more than 3,000 sales of night vision systems in the first five months of operation with over 2,000 of those sales coming in the fifth month. The company was formed to provide the night vision and electro-optical technology to commercial consumers, first responder agencies, law enforcement professionals and elite military units around the world.

About the Armasight Team

Armasight is a relatively small company with only 13 employees responsible for sourcing, marketing and shipping products worldwide. Those working for the company have held positions with such impressive industry companies as American Technologies Network (ATN), Nivisys Industries, Litton Electro-Optical Systems and Insight Technology. In all, the team has an accumulated experience topping more than 140 years.

The number one priority for the team is to provide the best quality products as well as the best service. Value in products is also a top priority when sourcing new and innovative products. The products offered are those that include the latest technology for use in multiple applications.

Products Offered by Armasight

Knowledge of the industry and the needs of the consumers provide Armasight with the ability to know what clients are looking for and the products offered prove that knowledge. There are night vision systems available to meet the needs of most industry professionals. The products made available include:

  • - FLIR
  • - ITT Exelis
  • - Photonis
  • - L3 Warrior
  • - Surefire
  • - Nivisys
  • - Trijicon
  • - Norotos

More brands are added as they emerge as being at the top of the product lists for providing quality and value. These products include those that are specific to night vision, thermal imaging, direct view, illumination, flashlights, helmets & mounts and more.

Products range from hand held items including binoculars and monocular devices to gun mounted riflescopes to helmet mounted devices such as goggles and more. Products range in price depending on the available features, durability of the product and the technology used in the device. All products offered have been chosen specifically for the value they provide in any industry.
